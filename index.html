Gifford Wright Brooks

Adventurer, Learner, Cruciverbalist





My friends call me Giff.

These days, I live in San Francisco and work in fintech.
How did I get here?

  • Growing up in Baltimore, I became a fiercely loyal Orioles and Ravens fan. I attended Gilman School through graduation, where I played soccer and geeked out on the quiz bowl team. Although I've since lived in Philadelphia, London, DC, San Francisco, and soon Chicago, I always consider Baltimore home.

  • After Haverford, my aptitude for economics and problem-solving led me to Cornerstone Research, an economic consulting firm. During my four years there, I led teams performing complex analyses for fascinating, high-stakes litigation cases. I also enjoyed recruiting new analysts, ultimately leading the San Francisco office's efforts in 2015.
  • I moved to suburban Philadelphia to attend Haverford College, a tight-knit community of thinkers and doers. There, I studied Mathematical Economics, tutored other Economics students, and continued playing soccer. I even had the opportunity to spend my junior year studying abroad at the LSE.

  • I joined SoFi in early 2016, seeking to apply my financial and analytical skills in an operational role. I now conduct research on the company's loan performance and provide credit policy recommendations to the CEO, CFO, and Chief Risk Officer. I am inspired by the positive impact that SoFi has on its 200,000+ members' lives.
